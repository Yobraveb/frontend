About Me

A passionate optometry student dedicated to learning and making vision health accessible to everyone

I am Elizabeth Njoki, an aspiring optometrist with a strong passion for vision care. As a medical student specializing in eye health, I am driven by the mission to prevent blindness and promote eye wellness in my community.

I strive to combine academic excellence with compassionate patient care, believing that quality eye care should be accessible to everyone. My goal is to make a meaningful impact in the field of optometry through both clinical practice and community outreach.

As a student optometrist, I am committed to the complete eradication of preventable blindness and work tirelessly to bring awareness and care to underserved communities.
